## AI Use Case Intake & Risk Triage

### Problem
Enterprises struggle to consistently govern AI use cases across business, legal, security, and compliance teams.

### Solution
This project provides a structured AI intake and risk triage workflow that classifies AI use cases, enforces approval gates, and creates an auditable decision trail.

### Frameworks & Standards
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework
- EU AI Act (risk tiers)
- ISO/IEC 42001

### Business Value
- Faster AI approvals
- Reduced regulatory exposure
- Consistent enterprise oversight
